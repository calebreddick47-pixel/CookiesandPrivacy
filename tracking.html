The Many Ways They Track You

These website use many tracking technologies to track users and collect their information online. This data is usually tracked in order to either improve the users experience or to better target ads.

the common tracking methods

  • Cookies
  • Tracking Pixels
  • Device Fingerprinting
  • Behavioral Targeting

What is the point of tracking?

  1. To make ads more personalized
  2. For website analytics
  3. To improve a users experience

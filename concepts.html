Key Concepts

These are a few of the key concepts related to cookies and online privacy:

  1. Cookies: Small files that are stored on your device by websites.
  2. Privacy: The Capability to control your personal information online.
  3. Tracking The monitoring of a users activity across websites.
  4. Encryption: making sure a user's data is secure by turning it into secure code.
  5. Third-Party Cookies: Cookies made by other parties outside of the website a user visits.
  6. Data Collection: Obtaining knowledge about a user and their behavior.
